Welcome Forbesian !

The goal of Forbes College Education is to provide quality education through educational innovation as a key to better lives. We are committed not only to provide access to a holistic education but also to strengthen our local and international linkages to maintain equal leverage in program advancement and student employment.

Embrace the Forbesian Journey: Where Knowledge Meets Experience, and Confidence Thrives

To be a Forbesian is a great experience. The community molds everyone not only in knowledge but also in skills. Additionally, FC offers not only different courses but also unforgettable experiences. The institution provides various extracurricular activities that encourages students in showcasing their talents and skills, supporting in their overall development. As one of the institutions here in Legazpi, FC guarantees that students are engaged not only in academics but also in socialization, and collaboration with professors who guide them to become better individuals, ready for the workplace as they enter the reality of being an adult. To be a Forbesian is to be yourself but with confidence.
