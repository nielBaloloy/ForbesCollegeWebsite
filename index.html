What is it like to be a FORBESIAN?

To be a Forbesian is a great experience. You get a chance to experience honing your knowledge and practical skills. You get to choose from different courses and experience amazing opportunities. Forbes College provides various extra-curricular activities which allow us to showcase our skills and talents, thereby ensuring our holistic development.
