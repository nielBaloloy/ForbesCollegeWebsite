The Resurgence of Forbes Scribe

The Forbes Scribe is the official school publication of Forbes College. It plays a vital role in keeping the student body informed about campus events, issues, and achievements. Basically, Scribe provides a platform for student voices as well as empowering young writers to hone their craft and share stories that resonate with their peers.
