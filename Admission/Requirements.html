Forbes College Department

Incoming First Year Student

  • Original F138/ Report Card;
  • Original F137;
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate (3 copies);
  • 2 pcs - 2x2 Colored ID Picture (White background); and
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (Original).

Transferees

  • Transcript of Records/ Certified True Copy of Grades;
  • Certificate of Honorable Dismissal;
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate (3 copies);
  • 2 pcs - 2x2 Colored ID Picture (White background); and
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (Original).

Senior High School

Incoming Grade 11 Students

  • 2 pcs - 2x2 Colored ID Picture (White background);
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate;
  • Original and photocopy of Form 137 from previous Junior High school attended;
  • Original and photocopy of Form 138 (Report Card) of Grade 10 signed by the principal;
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character from the previous school attended;
  • Photocopy of Diploma (Certificate of Completion).

Transferees

  • Transcript of Records/ Certified True Copy of Grades;
  • Certificate of Honorable Dismissal;
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate (3 copies);
  • 2 pcs - 2x2 Colored ID Picture (White background); and
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (Original).
