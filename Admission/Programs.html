College of Business Education

Overview

Forbes College offers a cutting-edge Marketing Management program designed to prepare students for successful careers in the dynamic and fast-paced world of marketing. This program integrates the latest industry practices with foundational marketing principles, ensuring graduates are well-equipped to handle contemporary marketing challenges and drive business growth.

Key Learning Outcomes

  1. Strategic Marketing Planning
    • Develop comprehensive marketing strategies that align with business objectives
    • Utilize market research to inform strategic decisions and identify market opportunities
    • Analyze and predict consumer behavior to enhance marketing effectiveness
  2. Digital Marketing Expertise
    • Master the use of digital marketing tools such as social media marketing content marketing, and email campaigns
    • Measure and analyze digital marketing metrics to refine and improve campaign performance
  3. Brand Management
    • Learn the art of building, maintaining, and enhancing brand equity
    • Develop brand strategies that resonate with target audiences and foster brand loyalty
  4. Integrated Marketing Communications (IMC)
    • Create cohesive marketing communication strategies that integrate various promotional tools
    • Craft compelling messages that effectively reach and engage target markets
  5. Sales and Distribution Management
    • Formulate effective sales strategies and manage sales operations
    • Optimize distribution channels and logistics to ensure efficient product delivery
  6. Consumer Behavior Analysis
    • Study the psychological, social, and cultural aspects of consumer behavior
    • Apply these insights to develop marketing strategies that influence purchasing decisions
  7. Market Research and Analytics
    • Conduct qualitative and quantitative market research
    • Use advanced analytics tools to interpret market data and drive marketing decisions

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Forbes College Marketing Management program are well-prepared for a wide range of careers, including:

  • Marketing Manager
  • Brand Manager
  • Digital Marketing Specialist
  • Market Research Analyst
  • Sales Manager
  • Advertising Executive
  • Product Manager
  • Public Relations Specialist

The Marketing Management program at Forbes College combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, providing a comprehensive education that prepares students for the demands of the marketing industry, whether aiming for leadership roles or specialized careers.

Overview

Bachelor in Public Administration aims to develop public servants with the required knowledge, values and skills to serve as professionals in government and civil society. It prepares its graduates for technical and administrative positions in government, including Foreign Service and Civil Society. It is a good preparation for those interested in taking up law. Graduates can look forward to executive policy-making positions in government, both elective and appointive, as well as executive and policy-making positions in civil society organizations.

Career Opportunities

Public Administration graduates possess a diverse skill set that can open up a range of career opportunities in the public sector, non-profit organizations, and the private sector. Here are some specific job roles that are commonly pursued by Public Administration graduates:

  1. Public Administrator: This role involves managing and overseeing the operations of government agencies or departments at various levels, such as local, state, or federal. Public administrators are responsible for policy implementation, budgeting, human resources, and ensuring effective service delivery to the public.
  2. Policy Analyst: Policy analysts analyze existing policies, propose new policy initiatives, and evaluate their effectiveness. They conduct research, gather data, and provide recommendations to government officials and policymakers. This role requires strong analytical skills, critical thinking, and the ability to understand complex social and economic issues.
  3. Non-profit Manager: Non-profit organizations rely on effective management to fulfill their missions. Public Administration graduates can work as managers in non-profit organizations, overseeing program development, fundraising, financial management, and stakeholder engagement. This role requires a deep understanding of the non-profit sector and the ability to navigate funding challenges and community dynamics.
  4. Program Manager: Program managers are responsible for designing, implementing, and evaluating programs that address specific social or public issues. They coordinate resources, monitor program performance, and ensure that objectives are met. Public Administration graduates with strong project management skills and a keen understanding of public policy are well-suited for this role.
  5. Budget Analyst: Budget analysts work with government agencies or organizations to develop and manage budgets. They analyze financial data, monitor spending, and make recommendations for budget allocations. Public Administration graduates with a solid understanding of public finance and resource management can excel in this role.
  6. Intergovernmental Relations Specialist: This role involves facilitating communication and collaboration between different levels of government, such as federal, state, and local entities. Intergovernmental relations specialists help coordinate policies, resolve conflicts, and promote cooperation among government agencies.
  7. Researcher/Consultant: Public Administration graduates can work as researchers or consultants, conducting research on public policy issues, evaluating program effectiveness, and providing expert advice to government agencies, think tanks, or consulting firms. This role requires strong analytical and communication skills.

These are just a few examples of the many career paths available to Public Administration graduates. The field offers a wide range of opportunities to contribute to public service, policy development, and societal improvement.

College of Information Technology Education

Overview

Forbes College’s Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge for a successful career in the IT industry. Our program includes courses in Web Development, System Development, and App Development, providing hands-on experience in creating and maintaining cutting-edge digital solutions. Students will also delve into UI/UX Design to master the art of crafting user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. System Analysis and Design and Database Management courses ensure a robust understanding of the architecture and organization of IT systems. Furthermore, Graphic Designing hones creative skills essential for visual communication, while Network and System Administration prepares students to manage and secure IT infrastructures effectively. Graduates will emerge as versatile IT professionals ready to tackle the dynamic challenges of the technology sector.
